Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?
We are open from 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily. Emergency services are available 24/7.
Do you offer grooming services?
Yes, we offer full grooming including baths, trimming, brushing & spa care.
How can I book a package?
You can book from the Packages page or contact us directly through the booking form.
Are vaccinations required for boarding?
Yes, up-to-date vaccinations are mandatory for safety and health.
Can I visit before booking?
Yes! You are welcome to tour the facility and meet our friendly staff.
