About Us

Who We Are

Loving Homes is a dedicated pet care and adoption service located in Hong Kong. Our mission is to rescue, care for, and provide loving homes for pets in need. Every animal deserves a safe, healthy, and happy environment — and we make that possible.

Our Goals

  • Provide a safe home for abandoned animals
  • Offer professional care, playtime, and training
  • Match pets with responsible, loving families
  • Promote animal welfare in the community

Why Choose Us?

We go the extra mile. From professional facilities to caring staff, we ensure your furry friends enjoy comfort, fun, and love every day. Adoption or boarding — we treat pets like family.
